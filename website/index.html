Sustainability

Use energy insight to lower your footprint.

Powercalc makes power patterns visible across your home, helping you choose smarter devices, reduce waste, and avoid extra hardware where virtual sensors are accurate enough.

  • 1 Reduce unnecessary plugsUse virtual sensors where estimates are good enough, and reserve physical meters for devices that need exact measurements.
  • 2 Understand power patternsStandby power, room totals, and utility meters make recurring waste easier to find, compare, and automate away.
  • 3 Choose efficient devicesThe profile library can support purchasing decisions by comparing devices on measured or estimated consumption.
